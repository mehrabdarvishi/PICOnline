Empower your research and streamline your data analysis with PICOnline, the user-friendly, online platform for calculating stress tolerance and susceptibility indices in crops.

PICOnline is a user-friendly online software that calculates common stress tolerance and susceptibility indices for various crop traits. It is an essential tool for agronomists and breeders who are dealing with large volumes of data and need to quickly and easily evaluate the stress tolerance of plant genotypes.

PICOnline is a web-based program that provides access to a variety of well-established yield-based indices for evaluating stress tolerance in crops. It is the perfect tool for agronomists and breeders who need to screen large volumes of data for the most tolerant genotypes.

PICOnline calculates the following indices:

  • Tolerance index (TOL)
  • Relative stress index (RSI)
  • Mean productivity (MP)
  • Harmonic mean (HM)
  • Yield stability index (YSI)
  • Geometric mean productivity (GMP)
  • Stress susceptibility index (SSI)
  • Stress tolerance index (STI)
  • Yield index (YI)

In addition to calculating these indices, PICOnline can also:

  • Calculate their ranking patterns
  • Estimate the relative frequency for each index
  • Create heat maps based on Pearson’s and Spearman’s rank‐order correlation analyses
  • Render three‐dimensional plots based on both yield performances and each index to separate entry genotypes into Fernandez’s groups (A, B, C, and D)
  • Perform principal component analysis

PICOnline is accurate and easy to use. It is accessible from any device with a web browser, and there is no need to download or install any software.
